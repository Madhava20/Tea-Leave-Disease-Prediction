About Us

Our Mission

...


Welcome to the Tea Leaves Disease Detection Project, where we leverage the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) to revolutionize the way we monitor and manage diseases affecting tea plants. Our mission is to empower tea farmers with cutting-edge technology to enhance crop health, yield, and sustainability.

Our Vision...

At this website, we envision a future where precision agriculture meets traditional tea cultivation, fostering a harmonious balance between technology and nature. By detecting diseases early and accurately, we aim to contribute to a thriving tea industry while promoting environmental stewardship.
