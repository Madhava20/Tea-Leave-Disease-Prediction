OUR PROJECT ON TEA LEAVES DISEASE

Tea leaves pictures can be used for the prediction of various tea leaves diseases which causes huge loss in agricultural system every year. The proposed system - Tea leaves Image Processing System helps us to the analysis of leaves and thereby help us in predicting various leaves diseases. The input to the proposed system is the Tea leaves Image. The leaves portion is segmented and leaves color, shape and texture features are extracted and taken together to form a feature vector and then analysis of leaves are done which is used for the diagnosis of various tea leaves diseases. This proposed system will help the farmers in the early diagnosis of diseases.

The tea leaf diseases we will be discussing are Anthracnose , Algal Leaf , Bird Eye Spot , Brown blight , Gray Light , Red Leaf Spot and White Spot.

Tea Leaf Diseases

Anthracnose

Anthracnose is a fungal disease causing dark lesions on plants. Prevent it by pruning affected parts, improving air circulation, and managing water. Fertilize with balanced nutrients and organic matter to boost plant health and resistance. In severe cases, copper-based fungicides may be necessary.

Algal Leaf

Algal leaf disease manifests as greenish or brownish spots on leaves due to algae growth. Prevent it by promoting good drainage, avoiding overwatering, and maintaining proper spacing for air circulation. Use balanced fertilizers with proper nutrient levels to discourage algal proliferation. Additionally, copper-based fungicides can help control severe cases.

Bird Eye Spot

Bird's Eye Spot is a fungal disease causing small, dark spots on leaves. Control it by pruning affected areas, promoting air circulation, and avoiding overhead watering. Fertilize with balanced nutrients to enhance plant health and resistance. Copper-based fungicides can be used for severe cases.

Brown Blight

Brown Blight is a disease characterized by browning and withering of plant tissues. Prevent it by maintaining proper plant hygiene, pruning affected parts, and ensuring well-drained soil. Use balanced fertilizers with essential nutrients to bolster plant health. Fungicides, especially those containing copper, can be applied in severe cases for control.

Gray Light

Gray Blight is a disease marked by grayish lesions on plants. To prevent it, practice good sanitation, remove affected plant parts, and ensure proper air circulation. Use well-balanced fertilizers to promote plant health and resilience. Apply fungicides, particularly those with copper, if the disease persists.

Red Leaf Spot

Red Leaf Spot is a disease causing red lesions on leaves. Prevent it by maintaining good plant hygiene, pruning affected parts, and providing adequate air circulation. Use balanced fertilizers with essential nutrients to support plant health. Apply fungicides, especially those containing copper, if the disease becomes severe.

White Spot

White Spot is a disease characterized by white lesions on plants. To combat it, practice good plant hygiene, remove affected parts, and ensure proper air circulation. Use balanced fertilizers with essential nutrients to strengthen plant resistance. Fungicides, including copper-based ones, can be applied if the disease persists.
